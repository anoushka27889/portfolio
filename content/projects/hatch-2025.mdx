# Hatch 2025: AI Sleep Agent

An intelligent sleep companion designed to help parents navigate the challenging world of baby sleep routines.

## Challenge

Parents struggle with inconsistent sleep patterns and need personalized guidance that adapts to their unique situation.

## Solution

We created an AI-powered sleep agent that learns from your baby's patterns and provides tailored recommendations in real-time.

## Impact

- 85% of parents reported better sleep within 2 weeks
- Average of 2+ hours additional sleep per night
- 4.8/5 star rating on app stores
